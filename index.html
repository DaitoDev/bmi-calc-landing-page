What your BMI result
means

A BMI range of 18.5 to 24.9 is considered a 'healthy weight.' Maintaining a healthy weight may lower your chances of experiencing health issues early on, such as obesity and type 2 diabetes. Aim for a nuturious diet with reduced fat and sugar content, incorporating ample fruits and vegetables. Additionally, strive for a regular physical activity, ideally about 30 minutes daily for five days a week.
